Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Listen, sometimes the simplest things in life are the best, and this sandwich is proof. It’s that classic, gooey comfort food we all grew up with—perfect for a rainy day or when you just need a win. No fancy bells or whistles here, just buttery bread and melted cheese doing their thing.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Generously butter one side each bread slice.
  4. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 slice cheese.
  5. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side up, on top cheese.
  6. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat with remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!
